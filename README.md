    _ SECT. VI. The Happineſs of Juſt Mens Souls, in the ſtate of ſeparation, vindicated.

      * An Appendix to the 8th. Section, being a Character of that Juſt Perſon whoſe Soul ſhall ſhortly be ſo Happy.
